        <p>Why contact me instead of a big-name company like XYZ? Here’s why:</p>

        <ul class="spaced-list">
          <li><b>You know exactly who’s doing the work</b>.  If you hire me, I
          do the work.  With large firms, you never know who’s behind the
          scenes. Sometimes it’s an expert; other times, it’s a freelancer hired
          through a chain of subcontractors.  The face of the company might only
          touch 5% of the project.</li>
          
          <li><b>If we’re not a fit, I’ll tell you</b>. If we can’t agree on the
          timeline or budget, I won’t waste your time. I’ll gladly refer you to
          someone I trust who can get the job done.</li>
          
          <li><b>No hidden costs</b> There’s no budget drain going to managers,
          marketers, sales teams, or overhead. What you pay goes directly to the
          person doing the work &mdash; me. While my hourly rate might seem
          higher than a salaried employee’s, it’s often a fraction of what an
          agency would charge for the same results.</li>
          
          <li><b>Quick and clear value</b>. I prefer short, focused engagements
          to get you exactly what you need. If things go well and we’re both
          happy, we continue. No pushy retainers &mdash; frankly, I don’t take
          on many at a time anyway.</li>
        </ul>
